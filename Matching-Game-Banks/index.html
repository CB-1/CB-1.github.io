Game Rules:

This section is a full, detailed description of how to play the Matching Game. It includes information regarding the game board, skill levels, score board navigation, and scoring.

The Game Board:

  • The board includes 16 cards shuffled at the start of each game.
  • Each card has a hidden symbol that appears when you click it.
  • There are 8 different symbols in all: , , , , , , , and .
  • Each symbol appears on two separate cards, resulting in 8 pairs of matching cards.

Skill Levels:

  • There are 4 skill levels: easy, moderate, difficult, and impossible.
  • Choose a skill level by clicking on the "Skill Level" drop down menu.
  • The 'easy' level allows you 60 minutes and 100 moves.
  • The 'moderate' level allows you 30 minutes and 50 moves.
  • The 'difficult' level allows you 10 minutes and 25 moves.
  • The 'impossible' level allows you 5 minutes and 10 moves.

Navigation:

  • When you are ready to play, click the play icon .
  • To pause the game, click the pause icon .
  • To resume play, click the play icon again.
  • To start a new game, click the replay icon .
  • To access the game rules, click the info icon .

Keeping Score:

  • You have a number of moves, stars, and minutes to find all 8 card pairs.
  • The number of moves and minutes will vary based on the skill level you choose.
  • Regardless of skill level, you start the game with 5 stars.
  • The number of stars declines as the number of moves remaining declines.
  • Once you start the game, your progress is shown on the scoreboard.
  • The first row includes the level you chose, the replay icon, and the info icon.
  • The second row includes these stats: correct moves, moves remaining, and stars.
  • This icon represents the number of correct moves.
  • The balloon icon indicates the number of remaining moves.
  • The star icon indicates the number of remaining stars.
  • The third row shows the time remaining.

How to Play:

  • The game board has 8 pairs of cards with matching symbols.
  • Clicking on a card reveals its hidden symbol.
  • Click on 2 cards at a time.
  • If the card symbols don't match, then both cards are hidden again.
  • If the card symbols do match, then both cards remain unhidden.
  • Continue in this fashion until you find all 8 card pairs.
  • Winning the game depends on how well you can remember each symbol on the board.
